# Likelihood Model

## Purpose

Defines the probability of a threat occurring.

| Score | Likelihood Level | Description |
|---------|----------------|-------------|
| 1 | Low | Unlikely |
| 2 | Medium | Possible |
| 3 | High | Likely |

---

## Examples

### Low
- Internal-only system

### Medium
- Public-facing application with controls

### High
- Public-facing application without controls
