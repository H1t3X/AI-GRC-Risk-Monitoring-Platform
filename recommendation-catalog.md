# Recommendation Catalog

## Purpose

This document defines security recommendations generated by the platform based on identified risks.

---

## Recommendation Register

| Threat | Risk Level | Recommendation |
|----------|------------|----------------|
| DDoS Attack | High | Implement rate limiting and WAF protection |
| Brute Force Attack | High | Enable MFA and strengthen password policy |
| Unauthorized Access | High | Review permissions and enforce least privilege |
| Missing Monitoring | Medium | Enable centralized logging and monitoring |
| Credential Theft | High | Reset credentials and enable MFA |
| Data Exposure | Critical | Restrict access and review data classification |

---

## Recommendation Priorities

### Immediate

Must be addressed immediately.

Examples:
- DDoS protection
- Credential compromise

---

### Short-Term

Should be addressed within planned remediation cycle.

Examples:
- Access reviews
- Password improvements

---

### Long-Term

Strategic improvements.

Examples:
- Security awareness training
- Compliance maturity improvements

---

## Recommendation Workflow

```text
Threat Detected
↓
Risk Calculated
↓
Control Identified
↓
Recommendation Generated
```
