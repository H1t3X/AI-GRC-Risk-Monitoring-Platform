# Asset Catalog

## Purpose

This document identifies and classifies the assets monitored and protected by the AI-GRC Risk Monitoring Platform.

Assets represent anything of value that could be affected by security threats, vulnerabilities, or compliance failures.

## Criticality Levels

### High
Assets critical to business operations, security, or compliance.

Examples:
- User Accounts
- Source Code
- Compliance Reports

### Medium
Assets important for operations but not business-critical.

Examples:
- Documentation
- Dashboard

### Low
Assets with minimal business impact if compromised.

Examples:
- Public marketing content

---

## Asset Protection Objectives

The platform aims to ensure:

- Confidentiality
- Integrity
- Availability

for all identified assets.
