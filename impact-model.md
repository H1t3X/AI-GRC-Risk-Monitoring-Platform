# Impact Model

## Purpose

Defines the severity of business impact resulting from a security incident.

| Score | Impact Level | Description |
|---------|------------|-------------|
| 1 | Minor | Limited operational impact |
| 2 | Moderate | Noticeable disruption |
| 3 | Severe | Significant business impact |

---

## Examples

### Minor
- Temporary inconvenience

### Moderate
- Service degradation

### Severe
- Service outage
- Data compromise
- Reputation damage
